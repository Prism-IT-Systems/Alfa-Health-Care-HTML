Introduction

Your Trusted Partner in Healthcare Excellence

Alfa Healthcare Supply Inc. is a leading healthcare medical equipment supplier and durable medical supply store serving Floral Park, Queens, and the Greater New York City area. With over 25 years of experience.

We are committed to helping patients, caregivers, and medical providers find high-quality wholesale medical supplies that enhance comfort, mobility, and well-being.

  • Insurance billing assistance

Our Mission

To provide healthcare professionals with a reliable, tech-enabled supply chain that ensures patients receive superior medical equipment exactly when they need it.

Our Vision

To remain New York’s premier DME provider, recognized for our commitment to clinical integrity, administrative efficiency, and compassionate patient care.

What We Do

Complete Healthcare Solutions

From mobility aids to respiratory equipment, we provide a full spectrum of medical supplies and equipment to meet diverse healthcare needs.
